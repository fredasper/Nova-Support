Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in classes?

To enroll in classes, log in to your student portal, navigate to Course Registration, select your courses, and confirm enrollment. Enrollment periods are typically open at the beginning of each semester.

What is my class schedule?

You can view your class schedule by logging into your student portal and clicking My Schedule. Classes typically run Monday to Friday.

What are the school policies?

Our key policies include 85% attendance requirement, academic integrity, professional conduct, and grading based on assignments and participation.

What are the academic requirements?

Academic requirements include maintaining a 2.0 GPA, completing required credit hours, choosing electives, and completing a capstone project.

How do I contact support?

You can reach support via email (support@school.edu), phone ((555) 123-4567), or during office hours (Mon-Fri, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM).
